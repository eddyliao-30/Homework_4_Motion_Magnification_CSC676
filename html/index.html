Question 2 (50pts) Problem Statement: In this problem we will investigate motion magnification in videos. Recall that position shifts in image space correspond to phase shifts in the frequency domain of the Fourier transform. This means that for two images, we can compare the Fourier transform of the two images to find the phase shift between the images. Amplifying the phase shift by a fixed factor in the Fourier transform frequency domain will amplify the position shift by the same factor in the image domain after we perform the inverse Fourier transform. We will use this idea to exaggerate the motions in videos.
